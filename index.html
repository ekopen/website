About

Hello! I’m Eric. Thanks for visiting my page!

I specialize in building and maintaining infrastructure for financial market interaction, a focus I’m deepening through projects featured here and on GitHub. I’m currently seeking part-time opportunities while completing my master’s this fall and will be available for full-time roles in 2026. My ideal position is an engineering role focused on real-time data systems.

I’m pursuing a Master’s in Financial Mathematics at the University of Chicago, graduating in December 2025. In summer 2025, I interned at Bank of America, supporting teams that built risk engines for trading desks. Before that, in the winter of 2025 I had an internship at Mizuho Bank, where I worked on machine learning approaches to building volatility surfaces. Prior to graduate school, I spent five years in data analysis and engineering roles at UnitedHealthcare and Agiliti Health. I hold a Bachelor's degree from the University of Minnesota, where I graduated in 2019 after studying Management Information Systems and Finance.
